OVERVIEW
NCCU Audio & Video Lab is composed of a group of selected professional filming students, which is affiliated under the Department of Radio & Television. The lab has been established since 1990 to provide the technical support and equipment for the filming courses, and also serves as a platform for selected students to have more filming opportunities to present in the filming festivals and other major shooting projects on campus. As a primary assistant in the lab, I took the responsibility to work on the visual design of the annual recruitment campaign.
